About the Conference

The objective of the conference is to exchange new knowledge and recent developments in computational techniques, mathematical modelling, energy systems, and more. It aims to provide a platform for engineers, researchers, and specialists from academia, research centres, and industry worldwide to explore the scope of applied sciences in emerging technology and computational intelligence for sustainable solutions.

The International Conference on Relevance of Applied Sciences to Emerging Technology (ROASET-II) is organized by the Department of Applied Sciences & Humanities, Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College, Ghaziabad (UP), affiliated with Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow, India.

This event offers a chance for young researchers to interact with eminent scientists and global researchers while discussing interdisciplinary topics like mathematics, statistics, computational intelligence, and renewable energy.
